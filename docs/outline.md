# Book Chapter Outline: Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics

## Course Structure (Conceptual Weeks)

-   **Week 1-2: Foundations of Physical AI & Embodied Intelligence**
    -   Introduction to Physical AI
    -   Concepts of Embodied Intelligence
    -   Historical context and future trends

-   **Week 3-4: The Robotic Nervous System: ROS 2 Fundamentals**
    -   ROS 2 Architecture and Core Concepts
    -   Nodes, Topics, Services, Actions
    -   ROS 2 Workspace and Package Creation
    -   Basic ROS 2 Publisher/Subscriber Examples

-   **Week 5-6: Digital Twin: Gazebo & Unity for Robotics Simulation**
    -   Digital Twin Concepts
    -   Gazebo: Robot Modeling (URDF), Simulation Environments
    -   Unity: Advanced Simulation, Physics, Visualizations
    -   ROS 2 Integration with Gazebo and Unity

-   **Week 7-8: NVIDIA Isaac AI-Robot Brain**
    -   Isaac Sim Architecture and Features
    -   Simulation for AI Training
    -   Basic Robot Scene Creation
    -   Implementing Simple Tasks (e.g., object grasping)

-   **Week 9-10: Vision-Language-Action (VLA) for Intuitive Control**
    -   VLA Concepts and Robotics Integration
    -   Speech-to-Text with OpenAI Whisper
    -   Natural Language Understanding and Action Generation with GPT
    -   Developing VLA Scripts for Robot Control

-   **Week 11-12: Humanoid Robot Development: Kinematics & Manipulation**
    -   Forward and Inverse Kinematics
    -   Robot Arm Control Examples
    -   Humanoid Manipulation Strategies
    -   Motion Planning and Control

-   **Week 13-14: Conversational Robotics & Human-Robot Interaction**
    -   Principles of Conversational AI in Robotics
    -   Speech Recognition and Generation for Robots
    -   Contextual Understanding in HRI
    -   Ethical Considerations in Conversational Robotics

-   **Week 15-16: Capstone Project: Autonomous Humanoid Blueprint**
    -   Autonomous Humanoid Concepts and Challenges
    -   System Integration and Design
    -   Capstone Project Blueprint Development
    -   Putting it all together: a functional autonomous humanoid system

-   **Ongoing: Safety & Best Practices**
    -   Robot Safety Considerations
    -   Best Practices in Robotics Development
    -   Ethical Guidelines and Responsible AI
